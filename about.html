Our Season

The Trailcats participate in the Kansas Interscholastic Cycling League which is a part of the National Interscholastic Cycling Association (NICA)

Our season starts with practices in late January. Our season runs through the end of May and includes team activities and destination rides, league races and adventure events, and more!
